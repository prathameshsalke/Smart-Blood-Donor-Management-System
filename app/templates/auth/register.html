Terms and Conditions
Blood Donor Eligibility Criteria
  • You must be between 18 and 65 years old
  • You must weigh at least 45 kg
  • You must be in good health at the time of donation
  • You should not have donated blood in the last 90 days
  • You should not have any transmissible diseases
Donor Responsibilities
  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Inform about any health changes that may affect eligibility
  • Keep your contact information updated
  • Respond to emergency requests when possible
Privacy Policy

Your personal information will be kept confidential and used only for blood donation purposes. We will not share your information with third parties without your consent.
